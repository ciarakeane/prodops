01 — What we do

Product Operations

MetaMask went from 0 to 1 last year, doubling our revenue and reaching parity with our competitors. This year, we are scaling from 1 to 10, creating a product that our users love and recommend. This change requires the product organisation to ship the right things, faster, with fewer misses.

Product Operations makes that possible. We remove the expensive friction: delivery risk that surfaces too late, decisions made without data, user insight that never reaches the roadmap, product managers doing admin tasks instead of focusing on the big picture.

Our team is organised across three pillars, with one goal - make every PM more effective:
